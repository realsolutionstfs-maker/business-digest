# Cookie Policy

**Last updated: 2026-05-07**

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or open an email. They help services remember your preferences and measure performance.

## Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `buttondown_session` | Maintains your newsletter subscription session | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Prevents cross-site request forgery | Session |

### Analytics Cookies

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `_buttondown_open` | Tracks email open rates | 30 days |
| `_buttondown_click` | Tracks email link clicks | 30 days |
| `yt_analytics` | YouTube video performance (embedded content) | Persistent |

### Third-Party Cookies

When you view embedded YouTube videos on our website, YouTube may set its own cookies. These are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

## Your Choices

- **Email**: Most email clients block tracking pixels by default. You can disable image loading in your email settings.
- **Browser**: You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. Note this may affect newsletter functionality.
- **Opt out**: Email privacy@businessdigest.com to opt out of analytics tracking.

## No Targeted Advertising

We do not use cookies for targeted advertising or retargeting.

## Updates

We will notify subscribers of material changes to this policy via the newsletter.
