# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-05-07**

Business Digest ("we", "our", "us") operates the Business Digest newsletter and related content platforms. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

## Data We Collect

- **Email address**: collected when you subscribe to the newsletter
- **Usage data**: open rates, click rates, geographic region (city-level)
- **Device data**: browser type, operating system (collected via email platform analytics)

## How We Use Your Data

- Newsletter delivery and personalization
- Content performance analysis
- Service improvement

## Legal Bases (GDPR)

- **Consent**: newsletter subscription
- **Legitimate interest**: content analytics and service optimization

## Third Parties We Share Data With

- **Buttondown**: email newsletter delivery and analytics
- **YouTube**: video content hosting and analytics
- **Anthropic (Claude)**: AI-assisted content generation (no personal data shared)
- **DeepSeek**: AI-assisted content scoring (no personal data shared)

## Data Retention

We retain your email address until you unsubscribe. Usage analytics are retained for 12 months.

## Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

- **Right to access**: request a copy of your data at any time
- **Right to deletion**: request permanent deletion of all your data
- **Right to rectification**: correct inaccurate data
- **Right to portability**: receive your data in machine-readable format
- **Right to opt out** (CCPA): you may opt out of data sharing for analytics

To exercise any of these rights, email: privacy@businessdigest.com

## Data Security

We use TLS encryption for data in transit. We do not store credit card information. We do not sell personal data.

## Changes

We will notify subscribers via email of material changes to this policy.
